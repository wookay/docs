Millboard.jl

https://github.com/wookay/Millboard.jl

Get julia data in a tablized format to arrange into rows and columns of cells. See also PrettyTables.jl.

Example

julia> using Millboard

julia> A = [11 12 13; 21 22 23]
2×3 Array{Int64,2}:
 11  12  13
 21  22  23

julia> table(A)
|   | 1  | 2  | 3  |
|---|----|----|----|
| 1 | 11 | 12 | 13 |
| 2 | 21 | 22 | 23 |
